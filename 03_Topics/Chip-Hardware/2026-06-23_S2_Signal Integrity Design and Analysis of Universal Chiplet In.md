## Abstract

In this paper, we design and analyze the Universal Chiplet Interconnect Express (UCIe) channel considering signal integrity (SI) in silicon interposer for advanced package. In a chiplet system, various chips from other vendors are integrated into one package using common UCIe channels. Silicon interposer is a promising advanced package that can achieve high bandwidth with high channel density. First of all, we investigate the possible interconnect dimensions considering a rout-ability in UCIe specification. Then, we propose transmission line-based interconnect structures with different signal/ground patterns for 2- and 3-layer routing in 32 Gbps operation.
