**年份**: 2025

## 摘要

With the rapid improvement of textile integration techniques, smart fabrics are emerging that integrate ultra-small-scale electronics (such as processors, sensors, and memories) into textile surfaces. To maintain the fluidity and comfort of conventional fabrics, smart textiles must use extremely compact components that minimize the number of area-hungry IO pads, which pushes in-textile systems toward disaggregated architectures with numerous tiny components that may span meters across a textile.
