## Abstract

In semiconductor manufacturing, as the silicon wafer diameter continued to increase, the challenges of handling the wafers in a manufacturing environment were manageable until the wafer size was transitioned from 200mm to 300mm diameter. For 300mm, handling wafers manually was deemed unmanageable at high volume due to wafer size, weight of the fully loaded carrier, safety and ergonomics, and fab productivity.
