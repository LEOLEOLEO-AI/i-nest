## Abstract

Vehicle detection and recognition based on Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) remote sensing images is of great significance in both civilian and military domains. There is currently a growing demand for real-time, precise, and reliable vehicle detection and recognition technologies due to complex and diverse application scenarios. In contrast to traditional close-range imaging, UAV remote sensing images present a more complex background, and the vehicle targets exhibit characteris-tics such as diverse types, multi-scale variations, and occasional dense distributions.
