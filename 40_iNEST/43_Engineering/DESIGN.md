# Crystal Cortex — 晶上大脑

## Style Prompt
A crystalline AI brain emerges from a deep void, fusing neuroscience with futuristic technology. Geometric crystal facets refract cyan and violet light, forming a luminous brain structure. Particles drift like synaptic signals through crystalline neural pathways. The mood is futuristic, immersive, and slightly mysterious — like peering into the birth of synthetic consciousness.

## Colors
- #050510 — Deep void background (almost black with blue tint)
- #00e5ff — Crystal cyan (primary glow, facet edges)
- #7b2ff7 — Electric violet (secondary glow, deep facets)
- #e8e8ff — Soft white (text, highlights)

## Typography
- system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif — Clean futuristic sans-serif for title
- Font weight: 700 for headline, 300 for subtitle
- Letter-spacing: 0.15em for title

## GSAP Signature
- sine.inOut, power2.out — Smooth, continuous, organic. Nothing snaps.
- Stagger: 0.08s between facet reveals
- Pulse: breathing scale 1 → 1.02 → 1

## Motion Rules
- Facets emerge one by one with staggered reveals
- Neural connections draw themselves with stroke-dashoffset
- Particles float with gentle sine-wave drift
- Title fades in slowly, centered below the brain
- Ambient glows breathe continuously

## What NOT to Do
- No hard cuts — everything blends and transitions smoothly
- No flat 2D graphics — every element must have depth via glow/shadows
- No generic blue-purple gradients without texture
- No text overlapping the crystal structure
- No static elements — everything breathes
