# 💡 Concurrent scale-free and small-world networks support criticality in cortical ensembles

**Concurrent scale-free and small-world network topologies are necessary to support self-organized criticality and neuronal avalanches in cortical ensembles, linking structure to optimal brain dynamics.**

## 与研究主线关联
线程: **criticality/topology**

This suggests that physical complex networks for intelligence (iNEST) should deliberately incorporate both scale-free (hub-dominated) and small-world (high clustering, short path length) connectivity to promote critical dynamics. Such topologies could be implemented in wafer-scale or SNN hardware to maximize information processing and metastability, potentially enabling emergent computation without explicit training.
