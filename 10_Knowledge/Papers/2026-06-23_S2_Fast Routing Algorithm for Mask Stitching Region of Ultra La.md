## Abstract

Interposer-based packaging has gained tremendous popularity in integrating advanced logic and memory chiplets for artificial intelligence and high-performance computing systems. The size of the silicon interposer is the critical bottleneck in improving the performance of integrated systems by mounting more and more advanced chiplets, such as high bandwidth memory (HBM). Nowadays, ultra large wafer scale integration is a popular alternative to integrate large amounts of advanced chiplets on a big wafer scale silicon interposer. However, wafer scale silicon interposers cannot be manufactured by one mask due to the reticle limitation.
