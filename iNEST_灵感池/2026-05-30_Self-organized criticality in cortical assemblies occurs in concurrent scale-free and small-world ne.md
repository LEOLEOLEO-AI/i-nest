# 💡 Self-organized criticality in cortical assemblies occurs in concurrent scale-free and small-world networks

**The paper demonstrates that self-organized criticality in cortical networks emerges specifically within combined scale-free and small-world topologies, linking network architecture to critical dynamics.**
