# 💡 Error and attack tolerance of complex networks

**The paper demonstrates that scale-free networks are highly robust to random failures but vulnerable to targeted attacks on highly connected hubs.**

## 与研究主线关联
线程: **复杂网络拓扑与计算**

For iNEST, this suggests that designing physical or neuromorphic networks with specific topological features (e.g., scale-free connectivity) could be a powerful strategy to engineer robustness. It raises the question of whether criticality or other emergent phenomena in our systems are similarly dependent on network architecture and attack strategy.
