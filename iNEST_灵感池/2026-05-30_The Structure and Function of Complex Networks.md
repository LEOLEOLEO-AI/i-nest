# 💡 The Structure and Function of Complex Networks

**The paper establishes that complex networks across physical, biological, and technological domains share universal structural principles, such as scale-free degree distributions and small-world topology, which fundamentally shape their dynamics and function.**

## 与研究主线关联
线程: **复杂网络拓扑与计算**

For iNEST, this suggests that the emergent intelligence in physical networks may be governed by universal topological laws. A key direction is to investigate if optimal computational or learning capacities in neuromorphic systems (e.g., SNNs, LNNS) arise at critical points in their structural parameter space, like specific clustering coefficients or path lengths. This bridges the topology thread directly with criticality and plasticity research.
